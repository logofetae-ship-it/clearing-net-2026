🧭 How Clearing works — 60 seconds
  1. Get results → check UCAS Hub status bar. A 6-digit Clearing number appears if you're in Clearing.
  2. Phone unis (below) → get a verbal "yes" → add ONE Clearing choice in the Hub. UCL = online form instead.
  3. If a choice comes back "Unsuccessful", add another. Keep going.
  4. Once confirmed → chase your CAS immediately for the visa.
⚖️ The firm / insurance rule
You can't swap firm & insurance. If UCL confirms, you go to UCL. Warwick (your insurance) is not in London — if you make Warwick but want London, use "Decline my place" in the Hub to enter Clearing, but only after a London uni says yes. Declining is one-way.
