# How to call & apply through Clearing — Nikita's London shortlist

> Only needed if you miss your UCL IB-38 offer. If you get 38, ignore all of this.

**Your details to have ready on every call:** your UCAS Personal ID (the 10-digit number in your UCAS Hub), your Clearing number (shown in the Hub once Clearing opens for you), your IB results, and a pen. You are an **overseas applicant** and **under 18** — say both up front; ask about international fees, a **CAS for the student visa**, and their **under-18 policy + guaranteed accommodation**.

## The 5 steps
1. **Get your results (6 July, Dubai time).** If you missed 38 and UCL didn't confirm, you're in Clearing — a Clearing number appears in your UCAS Hub.
2. **Call the university directly** (numbers below). Give your name, Personal ID, and the course. Ask if they'll take you.
3. **Get a verbal offer** over the phone. Write down who you spoke to + any reference.
4. **Add it as your Clearing choice in UCAS Hub** (only ONE at a time) — this makes it official.
5. **Then chase the CAS immediately** for your student visa (biggest bottleneck for a September start).
